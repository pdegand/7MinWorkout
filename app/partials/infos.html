7 MinWorkOut is a sportive schedule of fitness built by Hamilton's McMaster university in Ontario. A 7 minutes schedule as effective as 1 hour of jogging or biking according to the study.

This website is a virtual coach to help you following a 7 minutes workout, giving you informations about exercises to do and the break to take between them. This workout is quick, so please take a look at the exercises list before starting your first session. Then, you will know what to expect.
